ARS 2.x ONLY

ars_NTDeregisterClient(user, password, filename)

Cancel your registration with the NT client (ntclientd) application.

On success
1
On failure
0

Example: 

	ars_NTDeregisterClient("bob", "marley", "/tmp/mypipe1") ||
		die "ars_NTDeregisterClient: $ars_errstr";
