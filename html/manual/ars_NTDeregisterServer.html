ars_NTDeregisterServer(serverHost, user, password)

This function will cancel the NT registration for the given user. Use this when you are disconnecting from the NT sub-system.
On success
1 (True)
On failure
0 (False)

Example: 

	ars_NTDeregisterServer("myhost.myco.com", "foo", "bar") || 
		die "ars_NTDeregisterServer: $ars_errstr"
